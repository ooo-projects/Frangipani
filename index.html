Frangipani is a nebulous magazine which aims to become a landing place for ideas on literary culture in Lagos, Nigeria for a fixed period of time.

It is nebulous in the sense that the shape of each issue is determined by the wishes and capacities of its contributors.

It is interested in Lagos, Nigeria insofar as its initiator is from here and living here, hoping to grow the magazine via personal connections to artists living in the city. But it is closed to neither contributions nor interest from elsewhere.

It is temporary insofar as its focus is constantly changing, and it wishes to become dormant or defunct once it is no longer relevant.

The provisional format for the first issue is loose. It will include, in one form or another:
⁑ craft essays,
⁑ interviews,
⁑ poetry,
⁑ playlists,
⁑ ~fiction, and
⁑ a magazine within the magazine.

If you would like to be involved in any capacity in producing it, kindly send an email to me at smobidion@gmail.com, and we can talk. I will send you a mock-up of the first issue as an introductory measure - to guide our imaginations.
